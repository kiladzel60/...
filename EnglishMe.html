Percentages

What is a percentage?

A percentage is a mathematical concept used to compare quantities. Percentage means "per hundred". The symbol "%" is used to denote percentages.

How to calculate a percentage?

To calculate a percentage, use the following formula:

Percentage = (Part / Whole) × 100

Where:

  • Part - the quantity whose percentage we want to know
  • Whole - the total quantity

Examples

Example 1: There are 30 students in a school, 18 of them got an A. What percentage got an A?

Percentage = (18 / 30) × 100 = 60%

Example 2: The price of a product was 200 lari, with a 25% discount. What is the new price?

Discount amount = 200 × 0.25 = 50 lari

New price = 200 - 50 = 150 lari

Example 3: What is 40% of 15?

40% = 0.40

15 × 0.40 = 6

Practical uses of percentages

Percentages are widely used in everyday life:

  • In finance: Interest rates, discounts
  • In statistics: Data analysis
  • In science: Measuring growth or decline
  • In education: Grades

Useful tips

To calculate percentages, you can use a calculator or spreadsheets. It is important to remember that a percentage cannot be more than 100%, unless it is a special case.
